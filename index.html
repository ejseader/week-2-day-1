Flexbox Overview

    Flex works on an X/Y Grid

  • By default, a display:flex container is going to align children along the X axis (row). Horizontal placement is determined by justify-content.
    • You can also align along the Y axis. (align-items)
  • Flex boxes only affect direct children, but not beyond them.
